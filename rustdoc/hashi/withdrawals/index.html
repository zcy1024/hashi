MAX_ANCESTOR_DEPTH
Maximum ancestor depth permitted by Bitcoin Core’s relay policy (DEFAULT_ANCESTOR_LIMIT = 25). Bitcoin Core counts the candidate transaction itself in the ancestor set, so a UTXO whose existing unconfirmed ancestor depth is already MAX_ANCESTOR_DEPTH - 1 is the deepest we can safely spend.
